Grilled cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Directions

  1. Heat a medium sized pan on stove at medium heat or 350oF.
  2. Butter the bread and place buttered side on the pan with cheese on top and other bread covering that.
  3. Let sit for 3-4 minutes then flip. Bottom side of bread should be golden.
  4. Once flipped, let sit for another 3-4 minutes until cheese is melted and both sides of toast are crisp and golden.

Enjoy the best grilled cheese ever!
